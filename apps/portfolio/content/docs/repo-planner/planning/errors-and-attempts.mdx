## Minimal init -- what went wrong once

An early **`--minimal`** pass still wrote **`REQUIREMENTS.md`** / **`IMPLEMENTATION_PLAN.md`** *inside* **`.planning/`**, breaking the bare tree. **`usage.jsonl`** also recreated **`.planning/reports/`**. **Fixes:** **`runPlanningInitMinimal`**, **`REPOPLANNER_REPORTS_DIR`**, **`scripts/run-planning-cli.mjs`** defaulting reports to **`.planning-reports/`** -- full table in [getting started](/docs/repo-planner/getting-started) and [decisions](/docs/repo-planner/planning/decisions) **`REPOPLAN-REPORTS-01`**.
