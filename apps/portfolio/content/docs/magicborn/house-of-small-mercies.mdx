# The House of Small Mercies

No one storms the chamber with a sword. They arrive with seals, nursemaids, repeated phrases, and the confidence of people who believe paperwork makes harm smaller. The House of Small Mercies enters the story not as a shelter in the moral sense, but as the clean-faced machinery that absorbs a child once the settlement decides his mother is useful elsewhere.
