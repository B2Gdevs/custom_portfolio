# Morgana, the Sleeping Root

She wakes to labor in a chamber that still looks like hers, though the warmth has gone wrong. Later, in another strand of time, she is found asleep by a desperate man who learns that relics wake when skin touches skin. In the changed world, she carries her son through a city built on that theft and understands, before anyone says it plainly, that somebody has already learned how to live off her body.
