# Mordred's Tale ‚Äî Decisions

| Id | Status | Summary | Consequence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `MT-STYLE-DIALOGUE-FIRST` | `accepted` | Newer pages favor dialogue and interiority over long scenic description; enough setting to orient | Each page should have at least one exchange or reaction and one moment of interiority |
| `MT-SANDERSON-ADJACENT` | `accepted` | Tone and structure lean Sanderson-like: clear cause-effect, character-driven choices, propulsive plot | Magic and power feel rule-bound; character goals and costs stay visible |
| `MT-TIMELINE-JACK-IS-PAST` | `accepted` | Jack's chapter is the past (Ch2); it leads up to Morgana's current predicament. | `MT-CH3-INTERCUT-REALIGN` | `accepted` | Ch3 intercuts Morgana‚Äôs present (she‚Äôs taken) with Jack‚Äôs ‚Äúnot the same time‚Äù timeline; by end of **Act I** (last beat of Ch3), Jack re-aligns into the present and meets again. | Act I ends with convergence + ‚ÄúMorgana taken‚Äù + set-up for Act II orphanage route. |

| `MT-CH3-MORGANA-TAKEN-MORDRED-ORPHANAGE` | `accepted` | Morgana is taken during Ch3; Mordred is separated to the **House of Small Mercies** as an **infant**; Jack‚Äôs **network** closes the hunt by Act I‚Äôs last beat (`MT-JACK-PRE-CH3-LAIR-RHYTHM`). | Act II opens orphanage + bark / Midas beats. |
| `MT-FINISH-DRAFT-FIRST` | `accepted` | Get the full manuscript drafted; polish later. Content over polish. | Draft phase first; mt-polish deferred. |
| `MT-CHAPTER-AT-A-TIME` | `accepted` | Execution mode is chapter-at-a-time: draft full chapter, then one chapter-level rework pass before moving on. | Prevents page-level perfectionism from stalling completion. |
| `MT-CHAPTER-DONE-CHECKLIST` | `accepted` | A chapter is only marked done when the Chapter Done Checklist passes (beats landed, causality, motives, canon continuity, handoff, read-through, build check). | Gives objective completion gate for progress tracking. |
| `MT-MAGIC-NOT-FREEFLOWING-CH3` | `accepted` | In Ch3 / Act I, magic has not kicked back to free-flowing. | `MT-MORGANA-POWERLESS-WITHOUT-CHILD` | `accepted` | Morgana is **powerless without** Mordred in reach (or when separated); Mordred is the **well** she conducts (`MT-MORDRED-MAGIC-WELL`). | Ch3 vulnerability + separation stakes. |
| `MT-MERLIN-AT-CORE` | `accepted` | Deeper in the lair: core of the tree. Merlin there, stopped the tree's growth from within; doesn't know time passed. Opening that area kick-starts free-flowing magic in the world. Merlin thought it was "just an experiment." | Big story beat: when opened, magic returns globally; Jack (or others) have reason to go deeper. |
| `MT-HALF-SISTER` | `accepted` | Mordred‚Äôs sibling is his **half-sister** ‚Äî created by the empire using Morgana + a subordinate (same method as Jack in Ch2). Raised by Subordinate‚Äôs family; used as a tool to power relics until Merlin (~14). | Act II‚ÄìIII; she hates Jack and the empire after. |
| `MT-SUBORDINATE-ACT-I` | `accepted` | We **meet the subordinate** in Act I ‚Äî Jack‚Äôs **right-hand man**; he will father the half-sister with Morgana. **Morally grey** compared to Jack; doesn‚Äôt always believe in Jack‚Äôs methods but does as he is told. | Empire and half-sister set-up; contrast with Jack. |
| `MT-DEFAULT-CHARACTER-NAMES` | `accepted` | **Working names** (Arthurian/medieval flavor): Subordinate = **Owain**; half-sister = **Enid**; her daughter = **Gwyneth**. Docs may still say ‚ÄúSubordinate / half-sister / daughter‚Äù for role. | Prose and dialogue use Owain, Enid, Gwyneth; cast table on [state](/docs/books/mordreds-tale/planning/state). |
| `MT-SIBLING-NOT-BORN-YET` | `accepted` | At Mordred‚Äôs orphanage placement (Act I end), the half-sister has not been born yet. | Act II opens Mordred-alone; half-sister thread begins after her birth. |
| `MT-HALF-SISTER-RAISED-BY-SUBORDINATE` | `accepted` | The half-sister is **raised by the Subordinate‚Äôs family** (not directly by Jack). | She has a ‚Äúhouse‚Äù and status; arranged marriage follows. |
| `MT-ARRANGED-MARRIAGE-GENERAL` | `accepted` | By the time of the castle meeting (Act II), the half-sister is **arranged to be married to a general** in Jack‚Äôs army. | Stakes and position in empire; can inform the hallway scene. |
| `MT-CASTLE-MEETING-AGES` | `accepted` | Their **initial meeting** (castle hallway) is when Mordred is **15** and the half-sister is **14**. | Lock ages for that scene. |
| `MT-MERLIN-WHEN-HALF-SISTER-14` | `accepted` | **Merlin is found at the end of Act II** when the **half-sister is still 14** ‚Äî roughly around when Mordred and she meet the first time. Jack discovers him at the core; opening it kick-starts magic, tree grows, magic fills the world; empire threatened. That discovery is the **act break** closing Act II. | Not 30 years after Act I; Act II spans to ~age 15/14. |
| `MT-ACT-III-FAST-FORWARD` | `accepted` | **Act III fast-forwards** from the end of Act II to the conflict. We jump to when the southern war is underway; Mordred and his half-sister **remeet** there (with her daughter). | Act III does not span the full gap; it opens in the conflict era. |
| `MT-HALF-SISTER-POWER-SOURCE` | `accepted` | After Morgana is taken, Jack maintains power by using the **half-sister** (via touch) to charge relics ‚Äî same as sleeping Morgana ‚Äî until Merlin is found (when she is ~14). | She is both character and resource until the act break. |
| `MT-TONE-MORALLY-GREY` | `accepted` | Audience should feel **morally implicated**; tone favours **morally grey decisions**, **abrupt rash decisions**, **betrayal**, **submissiveness**, **resistance**, **greed**, **karma**, **scheming**. No clean heroes; consequences land. | Unsettled, hope and dread mixed. |
| `MT-MAGICBORN-SERIES-FEEL` | `accepted` | **Magicborn-line** target feel: **darker**, more **shocking**, high **emotion**, **tragic** stakes, with characters still **powerful** and **surviving** ‚Äî not nihilism for its own sake. **Moral dilemmas**, **ugly choices**, and **controversial dialogue** are welcome when they serve **cost**, **survivorship**, and **cause‚Äìeffect** (see `MT-SANDERSON-ADJACENT`). | Beat workshops and draft passes explicitly ask: what is the **jeopardy**, the **taboo**, the **price**, and who **walks out scarred but alive**? |
| `MT-BOOK1-BEATS-PASS-BEFORE-CH3` | `accepted` | Book 1 **execution order**: run **`mt-beats-pass-01`** (beat-lock **Ch6‚Äì14** + closeout + FQ sweep) **before** `mt-act1-02` / `mt-act1-03`. **Ch4‚Äì5** chapter rework runs **after** beat closeout so the Act II‚ÄìIII spine matches locked choices; **Ch3** returns **last** among these gates so clay-cord / separation can align with the full forward map. | Ch4 rework may note **Ch3 bridges TBD** until Ch3 pass; registry dependencies follow [task registry](/docs/books/mordreds-tale/planning/task-registry). |
| `MT-CH6-POV-OWAIN` | `accepted` | **Chapter 6 ‚Äî *The Second Well*:** tight third on **Owain** (guilt, ‚Äúhero‚Äù rot, institutional self-justification). Morgana‚Äôs interior may surface only as **Owain reads her** (face, breath, refusal) ‚Äî not her POV. | Ch6 draft and rework stay in Owain‚Äôs lens unless a later pass explicitly revisits. |
| `MT-MORGANA-SILENCE-OWAIN-COERCION` | `accepted` | In the **Owain‚ÄìMorgana / second-well** thread, Morgana does **not** speak to Owain ‚Äî **no** curse, **no** bargain line, **no** scripted plea that gives him a ‚Äúdialogue‚Äù to hide behind. Silence is **resistance**; any speech to **others** later is unchanged by this lock. | Ch6 avoids the ‚Äúone killer line from Morgana‚Äù beat; cost lives in **what Owain does** and **what he cannot un-know**. |
| `MT-TANYA-SUSPECTS-NEVER-FULL-KNOWLEDGE` | `accepted` | **Tanya** never gets **full** confirmation of Owain‚Äôs Jack/Morgana complicity (second well, keys, what happens on those nights). Suspicion **sharpens** after a **pattern**: Owain **gone most nights** for a **while** ‚Äî timing, body, lies, warmth gone ‚Äî and that **suspected-not-known** gap stays a **domestic horror** engine. | Ch6+ home beats: **subtext**, not confession; no full reveal to Tanya. |
| `MT-ENID-COVER-STORY-JACK-TO-TANYA` | `accepted` | When **Enid** enters Owain‚Äôs household, the **story to Tanya** (and the plausible public face) is that Enid is **Jack‚Äôs bastard**; **Owain** raises her because **Jack will not bother with fathering** / doesn‚Äôt want the bother. **Biological truth** remains **Owain + Morgana** per `MT-MORGANA-COUNCIL-COLLUSION` / `MT-HALF-SISTER`. | Aligns with `MT-TANYA-OWAIN-HOUSEHOLD` for Enid‚Äôs childhood fear of Tanya; Tanya‚Äôs **resentment/suspicion** can target the **Jack** story as well as Owain‚Äôs absences. |
| `MT-CH6-COERCION-PROCEDURE-EXPLICIT` | `accepted` | **Ch6 ‚Äî second well:** combine **procedure/consequence** framing (keys, orders, Jack as **auditor**, Owain‚Äôs interior split, marriage cost) with **explicit** on-page **weight** where it **lands cost** (GoT-adjacent: reader cannot pretend harm is abstract). **Not** a mandate for **gore** or **splatter** ‚Äî see `MT-BOOK1-VIOLENCE-CONSEQUENCE-OVER-GORE`. | Draft Ch6 with **institutional + visceral clarity**; avoid **titillation** and **torture rhythm**. |
| `MT-BOOK1-VIOLENCE-CONSEQUENCE-OVER-GORE` | `accepted` | Book 1 **preference:** **consequence**, **hard decisions**, **moral dilemmas**, and **irrational in-the-moment** choices that **spark conflict** over **extended gore** or **viscera spectacle**. Shock comes from **price**, **choice**, and **aftermath**, not from **lingering** wound porn. | Battle and horror beats stay **cost-forward**; explicit sex/violence when present serves **character and politics**, not **gratuity**. |
| `MT-CH7-POV-MORDRED` | `accepted` | **Chapter 7 ‚Äî *Devil in the Register*:** tight third on **Mordred** (toddler ‚Üí young child cognition). | `MT-CH7-JACK-GRILLE-NEAR-MISS` | `accepted` | **Jack** does **not** fully reunite with Mordred in Ch7. | `MT-CH7-NO-ESCAPE-ATTEMPT` | `accepted` | Ch7 does **not** use Alternative C (**failed escape**). | `MT-FQ-01-REGISTRY-CHURCH-WARD-JACK-PATRONAGE` | `accepted` | On **House / church paperwork**, Mordred is a **church‚Äìinstitution ward** (House of Small Mercies / pantheon-channel registry). **Jack‚Äôs** control shows as **patronage**, **favors**, **funding**, or **shell arrangements** ‚Äî **not** a clean ‚Äúfather‚Äù line on the form that fixes everything. | `MT-CH7-MERRIS-PROTECTOR-AND-DISCIPLINARIAN` | `accepted` | **Sister Merris** is **not** purely savior or purely villain: she **shields** Mordred from **worse** outside predators when it serves the House, and **disciplines** / **extracts** him institutionally (food, isolation, bark, registry). **Dilemma** beats over cartoon evil. | Ch7: punishment and ‚Äúcare‚Äù can **contradict** without resolving ‚Äî [`MT-BOOK1-VIOLENCE-CONSEQUENCE-OVER-GORE`](/docs/books/mordreds-tale/planning/decisions). |
| `MT-CH8-POV-OWAIN` | `accepted` | **Chapter 8 ‚Äî *Geraint‚Äôs Sister*:** tight third on **Owain** for the birth-in-household, **Jack** visits, **Tanya** poison, **Geraint** jealousy seeds, **bark** on **Enid**, and guided **metal** touch; **Tanya** horror and **Enid** interiority surface only as Owain reads them unless a later pass adds a deliberate split thread. | Aligns with Ch6 home arc and Owain‚Äôs split-love fracture; reserves **Enid**-limited child POV for later beats per roadmap. |

| `MT-LENGTH` | `accepted` | ~250‚Äì300 words per page; pages per chapter flexible (scene-driven); novel target ~80k‚Äì100k words. | Fix in draft or polish. |
| `MT-ACT-III-MORDRED-KING` | `accepted` | Act III: **Mordred overtakes Jack and Jack‚Äôs empire easily** and becomes the **new king**. Origin of Magicborn. | How he does it is a focus of Act III. |

| `MT-JACK-FATHERS-DAUGHTER` | `accepted` | **Jack** is the **biological father** of the **half-sister‚Äôs 15-year-old daughter**. | `MT-KINGDOM-OF-TARRO` | `accepted` | Jack‚Äôs order is not a separate ‚Äúempire‚Äù in kind ‚Äî it is the **Kingdom of Tarro**, one kingdom among many. | `MT-JACK-PRE-CH3-LAIR-RHYTHM` | `accepted` | Jack **escaped the lair months before** Ch3‚Äôs present. | `MT-OWAIN-WANTS-HEROISM` | `accepted` | Owain‚Äôs private hunger is to be seen (and to see himself) as a **hero**, not merely a competent enforcer. | Shades his compliance, envy, and the Enid household drama. |
| `MT-PANTHEON-POPULAR-DREAD` | `accepted` | Culturally, **almost everyone** is glad the old pantheon is gone: they remember (or were taught) **immortal cruelty**, favor-trading, and knees bent to power. | `MT-MORGANA-MERLIN-IMMORTAL-GIFT` | `accepted` | Morgana was born **mortal**, a **political gift** from another kingdom to **Merlin** ‚Äî immortality crafted for **his pleasure** and for her kingdom‚Äôs favor. | `MT-MORDRED-INFANT-ACT-II-OPEN` | `accepted` | At the **House of Small Mercies** handoff, Mordred is still an **infant**. **Midas / gold** is an **accident** of touch while he is a baby. | Act II opener age-locks; amulet beat stays dangerous. |
